Wine Types

There are two main wine categories. There is red wine and there is white wine.

As a general rule, red wine is mainly used for pairing in dining with meat. These meats can range from steaks to chicken, etc.

Also as a general rule, white wine is mainly used for pairing in dining with seafood.
